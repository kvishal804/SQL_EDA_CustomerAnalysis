Cohort = group of customers who joined in same year

Cohort analysis helps evaluate customer retention by grouping customers based on their acquisition period and tracking their behavior over time.

1. Retention Tracking
	Do customers acquired in a given period return in later periods?
2. Customer Quality
	Which year had better customers?
3. Business Impact
	You can answer:

Are we improving retention?
Are new customers worse than old ones?
When do customers churn?
